In four days, in a team of 5, we designed a shoes customization service. The added value of this service is that you can add fabric from a item of clothing that you own. Like any customization service you can change colors, but you can also add a picture or take one with your webcam to visualize your fabric on the shoe.
I developed a proof of concept for this project.
