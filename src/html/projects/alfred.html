Alfred

NodeJS, Javascript

ROLE:Developer

CONTEXT:School project

Alfred is a personal tie picker in the form of a terminal in shops.
It suggests a tie when you show it a shirt and advises you on what occasion to wear it and how to tie a knot.
This a proof of concept of this project.
