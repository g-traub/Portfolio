CONTEXT : School project

In four days, in a team of 5, we designed a shoes customization service. The added value of this service is that you can add fabric from a item of clothing that you own. Like any customization service you can change colors, but you can also add a picture or take one with your webcam to visualize your fabric on the shoe.
I developed a proof of concept for this project.

Color & textures personalization

I used CSS property clip-path to create masks over the different parts of the shoe. Then with JavaScript, we apply a change on the background of the mask when a color or texture is used.

Inputting files

To be able to add a picture of a fabric, I used a ⟨input type="file"⟩, that accepts png, and jpeg. With JavaScript I displayed the image as a background and saved it’s local path as a dataset in HTML. Then I resued the path to display the texture on the shoe.

Webcam & video

To take a picture with the webcam, I used WebRTC with a ⟨video⟩ and a ⟨canvas⟩ on top of each other. The canvas I used to capture the picture and after the picture is taken, I close the video stream and save the image to use it as a texture.
