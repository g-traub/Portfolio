UX case study

ROLE : UX/UI designer

CONTEXT: School project

In a group of 5, we redesigned the user experience of a salad bar focusing on it’s online ordering process. We audited the website and interviewed customers. We created a new and improved process for ordering and we designed the interface. We conducted user tests and tweaked the interface accordingly, and repeated the process.
